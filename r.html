Journal Prompts

  • How would you describe your relationship with parents, siblings, and other family members?
  • When you feel like you've been wronged in some way, how do you respond?
  • Is it difficult to ask for what you want and need? Why or why not?
  • When is your negative self talk most active? And when it is active, what does it say? Do you believe it?
  • How would you describe your role in most of your relationships? Is it a role you're interested in continuing? What do you think would happen if you switched things up a bit?
  • What would your best friends say if someone asked them to describe you?
  • What kinds of things worry you? How do you typically respond when you're worried?
  • What are you most proud of about yourself?
  • What relationship has been most impactful in your life? How so? Does this person know of their impact?
  • In what areas of your life do you find it most difficult to be compassionate towards yourself?

Reference: Therapy For Black Girls
